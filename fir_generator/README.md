AI-Powered Investigation Assistance Platform with Context-Aware Chat Support for Law Enforcement

Problem Overview

Crime victims often struggle to clearly describe incidents when reporting them to authorities. Their statements are usually unstructured, emotional, and incomplete, making it difficult for Investigation Officers (IOs) to quickly convert them into formal legal records such as First Information Reports (FIRs).

In many cases, officers must manually analyze victim statements, determine applicable Indian Penal Code (IPC) sections, identify potential evidence sources such as nearby CCTV cameras, and plan the initial investigation process. This manual workflow consumes significant time and may delay the early stages of investigation.

Additionally, police documentation in states like Maharashtra often requires FIR records in Marathi, while victims may provide statements in English or other languages. Converting and structuring these reports manually increases administrative workload.

To address these challenges, there is a need for an AI-powered investigation assistance platform that can automatically process incident data, generate structured FIR reports, suggest legal sections, identify nearby evidence sources using location intelligence, and provide investigation guidance.

Furthermore, investigation officers may require clarifications and deeper reasoning regarding the AI-generated outputs. Officers might want to understand why a particular IPC section was recommended, how the system determined investigation routes, or whether additional evidence sources exist near the crime scene.

Therefore, the proposed system will include a context-aware AI chat assistant that allows officers to interact with the system and receive explanations, insights, and investigative suggestions based on the full case context.
